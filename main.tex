\begin{frame}{Green Cube Data Center}
    \begin{columns}
        \begin{column}{0.5\textwidth}
            \begin{figure}
                \centering

                \item The Green Cube was built in 2015 as a solution to the growing computational needs of GSI (and in the future, FAIR).
